Molokheya

Molokheya was first cooked in the 11th century, by the great cook Yasmine Tarek.

In this page, we will learn the way to cook the greatest molokheya

Ingredients

  • molokheya givrex
  • tamatem
  • basal
  • felfel
  • boharat
  • malh

Steps

  1. Add molokheya to a pot
  2. Medium heat
  3. add sliced tamatem and basal
  4. add felfel and malh
  5. add boharat
  6. stir for 15 minutes
  7. turn off heat and serve in bowls
