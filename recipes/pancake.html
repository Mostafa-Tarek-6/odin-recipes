Pan Cake

Pancakes are quick and easy to make.

In this page, We will learnhow to prepare pancakes in 15 minutes.(recipe makes 14 pancakes)

Ingredients

  • pancake mix
  • milk
  • maple syrup
  • berries

Steps

  1. Add 660ml milk to a bowl
  2. add the mix to the milk and stir
  3. pre heat a pan with oil
  4. add the mixture to the pan
  5. leave for 1 minute on each side
  6. serve in plate and add berries and maple syrup
